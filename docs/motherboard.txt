= Configuration

First do a full restore.

Enable wake on lan.
Enable restore on power off.
Enable VT-D needed for gpu passthrough.

= Flashing log

Flash the newset bios version from https://www.msi.com/Motherboard/MPG-Z390-GAMING-PRO-CARBON/support#bios. The system rebooted 3 times after flashing but the fourth time was good.
